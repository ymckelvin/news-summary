Tool: &lt;click-to-play&gt; — a still that plays A progressive enchantment Web Component that turns this markup: &lt;click-to-play&gt; &lt;a href="URL to GIF"&gt; &lt;img src="URL to first frame" alt="..."&gt; &lt;/a&gt; &lt;/click-to-play&gt; Into a still frame with a click to play button which loads the GIF on demand. For when you don't want big GIFs to be loaded unless people want to play them. Tags: gif , javascript , progressive-enhancement , web-components
